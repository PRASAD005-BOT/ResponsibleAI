Common Challenges & Solutions

Common Challenges

  • Data Quality Issues: Incomplete or biased training data
  • Unclear Responsibility: Diffuse accountability for AI outcomes
  • Explainability Gaps: Inability to explain complex model decisions
  • Privacy Concerns: Unintended data exposure or misuse
  • Value Alignment: AI systems not aligned with human values

Effective Solutions

  • Diverse Data Practices: Implementing representative data collection
  • Governance Structures: Establishing clear AI oversight
  • Explainable AI Methods: Using interpretable models when possible
  • Privacy by Design: Embedding privacy protections from the start
  • Continuous Monitoring: Regularly auditing AI systems
