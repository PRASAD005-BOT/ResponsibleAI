Implementation Guide

Getting Started

How to Implement AI Governance

Follow these steps to implement AI governance in your organization:

1. Assessment & Planning
  • Evaluate your organization's AI maturity
  • Identify key stakeholders
  • Map AI use cases and their risks
  • Select appropriate governance framework(s)
2. Structure & Leadership
  • Establish an AI ethics committee or board
  • Define roles and responsibilities
  • Secure executive sponsorship
  • Create accountability mechanisms
3. Policy Development
  • Create AI ethics principles
  • Develop governance policies
  • Establish risk assessment processes
  • Define documentation requirements
4. Implementation & Integration
  • Integrate governance into development lifecycle
  • Train teams on governance processes
  • Implement monitoring and reporting
  • Create feedback mechanisms
5. Continuous Improvement
  • Regularly review and update governance frameworks
  • Conduct periodic assessments
  • Stay current with regulatory developments
  • Share learnings across the organization
